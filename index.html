How it works

Three steps to even.

From a pile of receipts to “who pays whom” in about a minute.

  1. Make a group

    Start one for a trip, a flat, or a dinner — then add everyone who’s splitting the costs.

  2. Log who paid

    Enter each expense, pick who covered it, and tick only the people who were in on it. Tally splits it evenly, down to the cent.

  3. Settle up

    See every person’s balance and the shortest list of payments that squares the whole group.

What it does

Small app, sharp on the details.

Fair to the cent

Uneven splits are distributed so the totals always balance — no mystery pennies left over.

Fewest payments

Rather than everyone paying everyone, Tally finds the minimum set of transfers to settle.

Copy a summary

One tap copies a clean breakdown for any person — or the whole group — to paste into chat.
